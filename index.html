About Don't Eat the Cashier

Don't Eat the Cashier is a dark comedy visual novel created by Miloff. Players take on the role of a cashier working the night shift at a seemingly ordinary gas station. The catch is that every customer who walks through the door is a monster.


Some customers are friendly. Some are dangerous. Some may become much more important as the story unfolds. Through dialogue choices and branching storylines, players uncover mysteries, build relationships, and discover multiple endings.
